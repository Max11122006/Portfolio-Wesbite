# 🚗 Honda Civic Engineering Projects

## Overview
This project represents a collection of hands-on mechanical and electrical work carried out on my 2006 Honda Civic. Rather than treating the car as just a vehicle, I approached it as an integrated engineering system — diagnosing faults, installing components, and understanding how subsystems interact in real-world conditions.

The work spans automotive electronics, diagnostics, maintenance, and system-level problem solving, providing practical experience beyond theoretical engineering.

---

## Engineering Approach

Each task was approached methodically:
- Identify symptoms and isolate potential causes  
- Break systems down into individual components  
- Test assumptions through targeted checks  
- Implement and validate solutions  

This structured approach allowed me to develop strong **diagnostic and problem-solving skills**, particularly when dealing with incomplete or ambiguous information.

### ▶ Custom Head Unit Installation
**(Fully installed and integrated independently)**

I replaced the factory head unit with an aftermarket system, handling the full installation process including wiring, mounting, and system integration.

**Key Work:**
- Removed factory unit and trim safely without damage  
- Identified and matched wiring harness connections  
- Integrated power, ground, and audio signal wiring  
- Ensured compatibility with existing vehicle electronics  

**Engineering Focus:**
- Electrical system integration  
- Wiring and signal management  
- Hardware installation and fitment  

**Outcome:**
A fully functional upgraded audio system with improved usability and sound quality, installed without external assistance.

---

### ▶ Amplifier + Subwoofer System
**(Full audio system build and configuration)**

Designed and installed an amplifier and subwoofer setup to enhance low-frequency audio performance.

**Key Work:**
- Routed power cable from battery to amplifier (including fuse protection)  
- Established proper grounding to avoid noise/interference  
- Connected signal inputs from head unit to amplifier  
- Configured gain and system tuning  

**Engineering Focus:**
- Power delivery and electrical safety  
- Signal integrity and noise reduction  
- System-level integration  

**Outcome:**
A stable, high-performance audio system with clean output and no interference issues.

---

### ▶ Fuel Pump Diagnosis & Replacement
**(Fault finding + full component replacement)**

Diagnosed and resolved a fuel delivery issue by systematically identifying the root cause and replacing the fuel pump.

**Problem-Solving Process:**
- Identified symptoms (engine struggling / not starting reliably)  
- Eliminated alternative causes (battery, ignition, etc.)  
- Narrowed fault down to fuel delivery system  
- Confirmed failure before replacement  

**Key Work:**
- Accessed fuel pump assembly  
- Removed and replaced faulty unit  
- Reassembled and tested system  

**Engineering Focus:**
- Fault diagnosis under uncertainty  
- System isolation and testing  
- Mechanical component replacement  

**Outcome:**
Restored full engine functionality and reliability, demonstrating structured troubleshooting and decision-making.

---

### ▶ Throttle Body Cleaning & Maintenance

Performed maintenance on the throttle body to improve airflow and engine response.

**Key Work:**
- Removed intake components  
- Cleaned carbon buildup from throttle body  
- Reinstalled and verified operation  

**Engineering Focus:**
- Engine airflow systems  
- Preventative maintenance  
- Mechanical system efficiency  

**Outcome:**
Improved throttle response and smoother engine performance.

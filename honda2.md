# 🚗 Honda Civic Engineering Projects

## Overview
This project represents a collection of hands-on mechanical and electrical work carried out on my 2006 Honda Civic. I approached the car as an integrated engineering system, focusing on diagnosing faults, understanding subsystem interactions, and implementing practical solutions.

Rather than following guides blindly, each task involved breaking down problems, testing assumptions, and applying structured reasoning to arrive at a working solution. This project highlights my ability to apply engineering thinking in real-world, imperfect conditions.

---

## Engineering Approach

Across all work, I followed a consistent methodology:

- **Problem Identification** — Understanding symptoms and defining the issue clearly  
- **System Breakdown** — Isolating subsystems (electrical, fuel, airflow, etc.)  
- **Hypothesis Testing** — Eliminating potential causes step-by-step  
- **Implementation** — Carrying out the fix or installation  
- **Validation** — Testing to confirm the issue was resolved  

This approach significantly improved my ability to diagnose faults efficiently and work with complex systems.

### Overview
Designed and installed a complete amplifier and subwoofer system to enhance the vehicle’s audio performance. The installation required careful handling of power delivery, signal routing, and system tuning.

### Engineering Focus

- Integration of multiple components into a single system  

### Outcome

<summary><strong>Fuel Pump Diagnosis & Replacement</strong> (Click to expand)</summary>

### Overview
Diagnosed and resolved a fuel delivery issue by identifying a failing fuel pump and replacing it. This task required systematic fault finding rather than trial-and-error replacement.

### Problem-Solving Process
- Observed symptoms such as unreliable starting and inconsistent engine behaviour  
- Considered multiple potential causes including battery, ignition system, and fuel delivery  
- Eliminated non-related systems through logical testing and observation  
- Narrowed the issue down to the fuel pump based on process of elimination  

### Key Work
- Accessed the fuel pump assembly through the vehicle interior  
- Removed the faulty pump unit carefully  
- Installed a replacement pump and reassembled the system  
- Tested the system to confirm correct fuel delivery and engine performance  

### Engineering Focus
- Structured fault diagnosis under uncertainty  
- Logical elimination of variables  
- Mechanical system access and component replacement  

### Outcome
Successfully restored engine reliability, demonstrating strong problem-solving ability and a methodical engineering approach.
